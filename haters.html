A Message To The Haters

  

It has come to my attention that there exists an "Ohhh!" cat page on the unofficial Shrek wikia. While it maintains relatively objective, it unreasonably labels the character as 'annoying' when this is anything but the case. The "Ohhh!" cat has only a positive presence in the broad landscape of the film, a role that is reinforced by their presence in the film's advertising material. If the character was truly 'annoying' as the author claims, then the film theoretically should have been a flop due to the heavy reliance on them; but it wasn't. To whoever is currently managing this article, please remove the 'annoying' excerpt from their bio.
