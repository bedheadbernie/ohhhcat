An Introduction

Throughout history, there have been cases in which a masterful piece of work was left forgotten and unappreciated until long after their inception. The "Ohh" Cat is one of these 'unsung heroes'. Debuting in the 2011 Shrek Prequel, 'Puss in Boots', this hilarious feline is noteworthy for their overlooked contribution to the film.

  

The cat performs a pivotal role in the film; as a reoccuring gag. They exclaim "Ohhh!" during (what they deem) embarassing and/or awkward moments. This happens numerous times, and it never fails to make me laugh.
